Visit Lima

Enjoy culture and food in one of the best yet underated gastronomic cities in the world.

Top three activities to do in Lima

Walk around in Miraflores

Walk in one of Lima's most renowned districts and while you're at it, take a peak at the nice Pacific ocean.

Visit Plaza de Armas at the heart of Lima

This emblematic plaza is home to many tourists who want to experience a bit of Lima's oldest colonial architecture. El Palacio de gobierno (the government's palace), Lima's main cathedral amongst other historical buildings can be found here.

Don't forget to eat ceviche!

You cannot and should not leave the city without trying the most emblematic peruvian dish: Ceviche!
